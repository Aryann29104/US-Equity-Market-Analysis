## Dataset Scope & Representation

This project uses a dataset containing **505 publicly listed companies**, which represents a **curated subset of the broader US equity market**.

In reality, the US stock market consists of **4,000+ listed companies** across major exchanges such as NYSE and NASDAQ. However, for analytical clarity and performance optimization, this dataset focuses on a **representative sample**, primarily covering well established and actively traded companies.

### Why this approach is valid:

* Many financial datasets intentionally limit scope to improve **data quality and interpretability**
* A focused subset allows for **clear sectoral, valuation, and performance comparisons**
* Common market benchmarks (e.g., S&P 500) also rely on **limited company samples**
* The insights derived remain **directionally accurate and analytically meaningful**

This makes the dataset well-suited for **market structure analysis, valuation comparison, and financial storytelling**, while keeping the dashboard concise and insightful.
